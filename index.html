Caitlín Nic Íomhair

About

This is the personal website of Caitlín Nic Íomhair. I currently manage An Siopa Leabhar in Dublin. Before that I taught Irish in Limerick, TCD, and other places. Before that I did a PhD on the poetry of Biddy Jenkinson at Trinity College Dublin, where I was a scholar. This page is still under construction, but at the moment you can read about some of my recent news. Feel free to get in touch with me at this address.
